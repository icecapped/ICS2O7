In the job market, System Architects are highly sought after and paid large 
amounts because of the amount of experience required to be an effective System
Architect.

Typical Wages: $80k-$120k per year
Median Wage: $102,000 per year

Example Employers:
-Oracle
-Microsoft
-RBM
-IBM
(Anywhere were there is a large IT system, but mostly the private sector.)

Outlook:
The future outlook for System Architects is good, because of the amount of
experience and specific knowledge required to do the job well. Alongside this,
it's also an IT job, which generally have good outlooks because of the increase
in technology use.
